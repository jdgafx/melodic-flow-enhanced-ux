## Appendix: Humanization Guidelines

### Gold Standard Principles:
1. **Results Over Features** - "Book appointments while you sleep" not "AI Chatbot"
2. **3-Second Rule** - Headlines communicate value instantly
3. **Conversational Authority** - Natural flow, no jargon or forced slang
4. **The "Reason Why"** - Back up claims with brief explanations
5. **Direct CTAs** - "See If We're a Good Fit" not "Contact Us"
6. **Rhythm (Burstiness)** - Mix short and long sentences
7. **Constraints:** No "stuff", no cocky tone

### Professional & Direct Pattern:
```
[Clear Benefit Headline]

[Direct statement of what it does and the result. No filler.]

[Specific feature] - [Business outcome]
```
