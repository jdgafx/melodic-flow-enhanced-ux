   - "ensure" ‚Üí "make sure"
   - "implement" ‚Üí "set up"
   - "optimize" ‚Üí "fix up"
   - "leverage" ‚Üí "use"

   - "intelligent" ‚Üí "smart"

2. **Add Contractions:**
   - "do not" ‚Üí "don't"

   - "it is" ‚Üí "it's"
   - "we are" ‚Üí "we're"
